Phil Kaye is a Japanese-American poet, writer, and filmmaker. He is the co-director of Project VOICE, an organization which partners with schools to bring poetry into the classroom. His debut book Date & Time was published by Button Poetry in the Fall of 2018.

"Repetition"

That's how gravity works.
You don't stand in love. You fall.
Further every season.
That's why the truth is harder to tell
every year you have more to lose.

Andrea Gibson is a queer American poet whose work focuses heavily on LGBTQ+, social reform, and political themes. Their discography includes: Bullets and Windchimes, Swarm, When the Bough Breaks, Yellowbird, Flower Boy, Truce, and Hey Galaxy; books include: Pole Dancing to Gospel Hymns, The Madness Vase, Pansy, and Take Me With You.
